CNN (Convolutional Neural Network)
**********************************
    because of ANN, below are the problems
    1) high computational cost
    2) overfitting
    3) spatial features loss

    to overcome above problems, we have CNN


Types of Images
***************
1) Gray Scale Images
    - 3 colors are possible
        1) black.   2) white.  3) gray
    
    - 0 - 255

    0 - black
    255 - white

    light gray - dark gray

    2D
    Ex.
        [
            [0,1,30,],
            [],
            []
        ]

    Normalization (Model will Train with highest performance)

2) RGB Images
    R - [255,0,0]
    G - [0,255,0]
    B - [0,0,255]

    Range : 0-255

    Numpy Lists (3Dimensional)
